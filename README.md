# 🩺 CareTrack AI 2.0

### "Smarter Symptom Analysis. Better Health Decisions."

CareTrack AI 2.0 is a next-generation AI-powered healthcare assistant designed to help users understand their symptoms, identify possible medical conditions, receive personalized health recommendations, and connect with nearby healthcare services.

The platform combines symptom analysis, disease prediction, doctor recommendations, emergency alerts, automated reporting, and health awareness features into a single modern healthcare ecosystem.
